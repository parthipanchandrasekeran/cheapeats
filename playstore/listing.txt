Hungry in Toronto but watching your wallet? CheapEats helps you discover amazing meals under $15 CAD - no more guessing, no more budget surprises.

KEY FEATURES

Smart Budget Search
Search for "cheap tacos" or "filling lunch" and our AI understands exactly what you need. Every restaurant shows verified prices so you know what to expect before you go.

TTC-Friendly Dining
Living that Toronto transit life? Filter restaurants by TTC proximity. We show walking time from the nearest subway station so you can plan your lunch break perfectly.

Lunch Route Planner
Short on time? Tap "Lunch" to get an instant plan with your best option and a backup pick. One tap opens walking directions in Google Maps.

Real Price Confidence
See exactly how reliable each price is:
- Verified: Confirmed by Google Places
- Estimated: Based on price level
- Unknown: Check before ordering

We never recommend a restaurant without being upfront about the data.

Filter Your Way
- Under $15: Only budget-friendly spots
- Open Now: Currently serving
- Near TTC: Walking distance from subway
- Student Discount: Places with student deals

Dual View Modes
Browse as a list or switch to map view to see all nearby options. Gold markers show sponsored restaurants, red markers show standard listings.

Save Your Favorites
Heart the spots you love. Create custom collections like "Lunch Spots" or "Late Night Eats" to organize your go-to places.

Deals & Specials
Discover daily specials, student discounts, and limited-time offers. Community-verified deals help you save even more.

Works Offline
Recently viewed restaurants are cached so you can check details even without internet. Perfect for the subway.

WHY CHEAPEATS?

We built this app because finding affordable food in Toronto shouldn't be hard. No sponsored rankings hiding the good stuff. No misleading "budget" tags on $20 meals. Just honest recommendations for people who want to eat well without overspending.

Whether you're a student stretching your budget, a downtown worker grabbing a quick lunch, or just someone who appreciates good value - CheapEats has your back.

PERFECT FOR
- Students looking for affordable campus-area eats
- Office workers planning quick lunch breaks
- Budget-conscious foodies exploring Toronto
- Anyone who wants honest price info before dining out

Download CheapEats and never overpay for lunch again.
