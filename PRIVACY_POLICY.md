# Privacy Policy for CheapEats

**Last Updated: January 27, 2026**

## Introduction

CheapEats ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **Purpose**: To show nearby restaurants and calculate walking distances
- **Type**: GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude)
- **Storage**: Location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers
- **Control**: You can disable location access in your device settings at any time

### Search and Filter Preferences
- **Purpose**: To personalize your restaurant recommendations
- **Type**: Search queries, filter selections (price, cuisine, TTC proximity)
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device only
- **Control**: You can clear this data by clearing the app's data or uninstalling

### Favorites and Collections
- **Purpose**: To save your preferred restaurants for quick access
- **Type**: Restaurant identifiers and custom collection names
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device only
- **Control**: You can delete favorites and collections within the app

### Offline Cache Data
- **Purpose**: To provide limited functionality when offline
- **Type**: Restaurant information, cached search results
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device only
- **Control**: Cache is automatically cleared periodically or when you clear app data

## Third-Party Services

### Google Maps Platform
- We use Google Maps to display restaurant locations and provide directions
- Google Maps has its own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- When you tap "Start Directions", the Google Maps app handles navigation

### Google Places API
- We use Google Places API to fetch restaurant information (names, ratings, hours, photos)
- This data is fetched in real-time and cached locally for offline use
- Google's Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Google Vertex AI
- We use Google Vertex AI for:
  - AI-powered search suggestions
  - Restaurant recommendations
  - Logo generation features
- Queries sent to Vertex AI do not include personally identifiable information
- Google Cloud Privacy Policy applies: https://cloud.google.com/terms/cloud-privacy-notice

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Payment or financial information
- Social media accounts
- Contacts or address book
- Photos or camera access (except for optional logo generation feature)
- Browsing history outside the app
- Device identifiers for advertising purposes

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., popular search terms in a city) for analytics purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify individual users.

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms
- Network requests to Google APIs use HTTPS encryption
- We do not maintain servers that store user data

## Children's Privacy

CheapEats is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored by the app (all stored locally on your device)
- **Delete**: Clear all app data through Android Settings > Apps > CheapEats > Clear Data
- **Opt-out**: Disable location services or specific features at any time

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
